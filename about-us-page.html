FAQ
How does Skill Bridge work with my school?
Skill Bridge partners with schools to integrate our skill-sharing platform with your school's network, enhancing the educational ecosystem and making it easy for students to connect.
Is Skill Bridge exclusive to my school?
While Skill Bridge is a global platform, our partnership with your school ensures that you have a tailored space to connect with peers, alumni, and faculty from your institution.
Can I find mentors from my school on Skill Bridge?
There are no formal mentorship but our platform enables you to connect with mentors from your school and community, including alumni and faculty who are eager to share their knowledge and experience.
Is there a cost associated with using Skill Bridge through my school?
Skill bridge is completely free for you and members of your community.
How do I join my school's network on Skill Bridge?
Simply sign up with your school email, and you'll automatically become part of your school's network within our community.
